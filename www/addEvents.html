Adding reminders

During this study, you will be asked to fill-in 3 kinds of forms.
  • Heart rate, Rating of Perceived Exertion and Weight. This has to be filled-in every training day, a first part pre-training and a second part post-training.
  • Warming Up Protocol: Once every 3 weeks
  • Psychological survey: Once every 3 weeks
Because it's very likely that you will forget to perform those tasks, we advise you to add reminders (or calendar events) into your smart-phone.
